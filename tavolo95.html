1995 Design: Achille Castiglioni. 1995 Production: De Padova. A round, square or rectangular table. The square and rectangular tables have four legs, the round table, three. The table top may be chosen from marble, wood or laminate. The frame is metal. The table tops can be protected by using a cover (flannel, tablecloths, mats etc.). The formal solution of an element in silver finished aluminium that creates a secure vertical connection between the wooden leg and the table top facilitates covering the table. All the edges are rounded off 45Â° and return to their maxim thickness (30 mm) at the most delicate point where the legs are attached. The cone-shaped wooden legs with a flat cut along the length which holds the aluminium joint are aligned with the table edges.
